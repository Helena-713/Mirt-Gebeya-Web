About Mirt Gebeya

Connecting Ethiopian Farmers with Consumers.

Our Mission

To create a direct digital marketplace that connects Ethiopian farmers, producers, and artisans with consumers across the country. We eliminate middlemen to ensure fair prices for both producers and buyers while maintaining the highest quality standards.

Our Values

Fair trade, transparency, and community support. We believe in empowering local producers while providing fresh, high-quality products to our customers. Every transaction supports sustainable agriculture and local economic development.

Supporting Farmers

We work directly with over 50 small-scale farmers across Ethiopia, helping them reach wider markets and get better prices for their harvest. Our platform provides farmers with market insights and technical support to improve their yields.
